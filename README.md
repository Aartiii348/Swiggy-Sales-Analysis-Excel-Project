# Swiggy-Sales-Analysis
This project analyzes monthly sales data from Swiggy to uncover trends, identify high-revenue periods, and derive actionable business insights. The goal is to understand customer ordering patterns, seasonal demand variations, and overall revenue performance.

**Project Objective**

To analyze Swiggy’s monthly revenue and identify key trends.

To understand seasonal demand patterns across months.

To assist business teams in decision-making around marketing, staffing, and inventory.

To build dashboards & visualizations for quick interpretation of business performance.

**Case Statement**

Swiggy’s business team wants to evaluate how revenue varies month-over-month. They require a structured analysis to understand peak demand seasons, low-performing months, and factors affecting revenue changes.

This analysis will help Swiggy plan:

Marketing campaigns

Resource allocation

Delivery partner staffing

Festival/season-based promotions

**Datasets Used**

File: Swiggy Raw Project.xlsx

Data Includes:

Month names

Monthly total revenue (Sum of Price (INR))

Example structure:

Month	Revenue (INR)
Jan	68,25,186.03
Feb	62,69,105.67
Mar	65,73,530.07
...	...
